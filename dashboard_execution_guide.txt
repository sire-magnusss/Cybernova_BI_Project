DASHBOARD 1: CYBERNOVA PULSE — SALES COMMAND CENTER

Dashboard question:
“What should Sales prioritise today?”

Purpose:
Help Sales identify:
- which warm leads to follow up
- which country is hottest
- which service should lead the sales conversation
- whether AI Assistant interest is converting
- where prospects drop in the funnel

Live Pulse cards:
1. New Warm Leads
   Latest 24-hour warm lead activity.
2. Demo Requests by Hour
   Current-day demo intent.
3. Live Lead Source Mix
   Country/service intent split.
4. Current Hot Service
   Most active lead signal.

Sales Insight:
Generate rule-based bullets from filtered data:
- top warm-lead country
- top warm-lead service
- AI Assistant to demo conversion
- funnel drop-off action
- best follow-up period/hour if available

Recommended Sales Action:
“Prioritise warm leads from [top country] showing [top service] interest.”

Strategic Analytics cards:
- Where did warm leads come from?
  Warm leads by country.
- What service should Sales lead with?
  Warm lead/demo demand by service.
- When are people most likely to request a demo?
  Demo request heatmap by hour and day.
- Are repeat visitors more likely to convert?
  Conversion rate by session cohort.
- Is the warm-lead pipeline accelerating or slowing?
  Weekly warm-lead trend.
- Where in the funnel are we losing leads?
  Index page → service page → demo page → demo submission.

Live Activity Feed:
Embedded inside Sales dashboard.
Use latest filtered rows, prioritising:
- is_warm_lead True
- demo_request
- contact_request
- event_signup
- AI Assistant interest

Show:
- timestamp
- country
- service_name
- event_type
- segment
- status_class

Evidence:
Sales Action Queue table:
- priority
- timestamp
- country
- service_name
- event_type
- segment
- recommended_action

Priority rules:
- High if is_warm_lead True
- Medium if segment is Product-curious
- Low otherwise

Recommended action rules:
- demo_request → Contact within 24 hours
- contact_request → Assign to sales representative
- event_signup → Invite to product demo follow-up
- AI Assistant interest → Send AI Assistant solution brief
- otherwise → Monitor session activity
